We respect and protect your personal privacy. This privacy policy explains what information the browser collects about you and why, and how it will be used and protected.

What information does the browser collect about me?

1. Location service is required, and the user records the location information of the photo taken.

2. The camera is required and the user takes pictures.

2. A microphone is required and the user takes a video.

How does the browser protect my privacy

1. All the photos or videos you have taken are stored on the phone and will not be uploaded.

2. We will not collect any information from you, including any data generated in the application.

3. We will not share any of your information with third parties.
