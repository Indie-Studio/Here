Here Ledger

Here Ledger is a personal accounting application with unique features and a simple and comfortable user experience, dedicated to making your accounting fun and easy.

Unique features

1. Display each of your consumption records on the map and clearly know where your money went. Details of the income and expenditure of each place, you can view historical consumption records.

2. Statistics function according to geographic location. Whether you are at work, on a business trip, or traveling, count how much money you spend in each region, city, and even province.

3. Fun and fun review function. Review the consumption process of today, yesterday, this week or this month on the map, animated display, and generate a review report.

4. Automatically synchronize with your iCloud data, no need to worry about losing data by replacing the phone or reinstalling.

5. Powerful statistical functions. Statistics by time, by location, by category, all matters.

6, custom classification, the system provides a default classification, you can modify or add or delete classifications.

More features are waiting for you to experience.
