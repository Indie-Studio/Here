Private Hub

Private Hub is used to encrypt and save your photos and videos.

Unique features

1. Photos and videos are encrypted and stored in the computer.

2. A password is required to access.

3. Decrypt the video when playing, and automatically clear the cache after playing.

4. You can export the decrypted photos and videos..

5. The application is not connected to the Internet to ensure privacy and security.
