We respect and protect your privacy. This privacy policy explains what type of information "Clear Widgets" collects and how it can be used and protected.

What information does "Clear Widgets" collect?

"Clear Widgets" will read daily steps, phone storage capacity, battery level, these information will only be displayed on your phone.

How does "Clear Widgets" protect my privacy?

1. "Clear Widgets" will not store your information anywhere except your phone.

2. "Clear Widgets" will not share any of your information with third parties.
