Privacy Policy

We respect and protect your privacy. This privacy policy explains what type of information Here Ledger collects and how it can be used and protected.

What information does Here Ledger collect?

1. Here Ledger will require a location service for querying nearby places. All records are stored in your mobile phone. Here Ledger will not collect your location information.

2. Here Ledger uses iCloud cloud service to synchronize user data.

How does Here Ledger protect my privacy?

1. Here Ledger will not store data on servers other than iCloud. The data synchronized by iCloud can only be accessed by the user.

2. Here Ledger does not collect any of your information, including positioning information and data generated in the application.

3. Here Ledger will not share any of your information with third parties.
